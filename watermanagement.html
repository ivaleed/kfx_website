Water Management

Kfx SCP architecture is modular and configurable, water management module provdes the rich feature to profile the water at various points of consumption like common/public utillization, resident utillization, forcast the water requirements with AI/ML model based on historical data.

Kfx SCP platform multi-tenant and multiuser, enables data metrics and forcast for ther target users, as well as block/zonal level perspective Kfx SCP automates the allied services like pumps to fill the storage tanks, idetifies the leakages. Cost of the consumtion is computed very intelligently with the data points.

Benifits

Borewell Pump Control

  • Monitor the water level in the Ground Level Reservoir (GLR)
  • Switch on one/more borewells automatically or at a pre-set time if the level in GLR is below Borewell ON threshold
  • Switch off borewells automatically on reaching Borewell OFF threshold

GLR/Sump Pump Control

  • Monitor the water level in the OHT (Over Head Tank)
  • Switch on the GLR pump automatically when the OHT water level is low.
  • Switch off the GLR pump automatically once the overhead water tank is filled.
