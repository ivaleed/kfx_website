technology

Internet of Things -IoT come to an age where it is indispensible entity across the industries and human life but the challenge is interconnecting the edge devices, sensors,actuators , PLCs and HMI , communication each other and cloud. Kfx Touck link-patented tech addressing the current challnges, enables all devices , sensors, edge devices and gatways to communicate over single wire in smart method.

Merits of the Kfx touck link scalable , protocol agnostic and software defined configuration which enables to control the latency and delays for each enitiy.
