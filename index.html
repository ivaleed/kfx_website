technology

Internet of Things (IoT) has come to an age where it is an indispensable entity across industries and human life, but the challenge is interconnecting edge devices, sensors, actuators, PLCs, and HMI, enabling them to communicate with each other and the cloud. Kfx Touch Link, a patented technology addressing current challenges, enables all devices, sensors, edge devices, and gateways to communicate over a single wire in a smart method.

Merits of the Kfx Touch Link include its scalable, protocol-agnostic, and software-defined configuration, which enables control over latency and delays for each entity. Kfx Touch Link combines multiple TDMA accesses on a common cable using FDMA. It supports a range of eight hundred metres, and adding or removing a device is much easier in IoT or any other communication edge networks.
